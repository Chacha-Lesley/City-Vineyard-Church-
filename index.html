About City Vineyard

Our Story, Beliefs, and Leadership

Home / About Us

Our Story

City Vineyard Church began in 2010 with a vision to create a place where people could encounter God's presence, experience genuine community, and discover their purpose. What started as a small gathering of families has grown into a diverse, vibrant community committed to loving God, loving people, and making disciples.

Mission & Values

Love God

We pursue authentic worship and cultivate intimacy with God through prayer, worship, and His Word.

Love People

We demonstrate Christ's love through authentic relationships, compassion, and radical generosity.

Make Disciples

We equip believers to grow spiritually and multiply disciples in their spheres of influence.

Welcome the Spirit

We create space for the Holy Spirit to move, heal, and empower in every gathering.

Reach Out

We engage our community and the world with the Gospel through service and missions.

Build Community

We foster authentic relationships where people belong, grow, and serve together.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God and our guide for faith and life.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, grow, and serve together.

The Kingdom

We believe in demonstrating God's kingdom through healing, justice, and transformation.
